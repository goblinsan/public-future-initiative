## Policy Summary

This policy proposes federal funding for a structured, multi-city Universal Basic Income pilot program designed to generate rigorous evidence on UBI's effects in diverse American communities.

## Key Provisions

- **Duration**: 5 years
- **Payment**: $1,000/month per participant
- **Scale**: 30,000 participants across 3 cities
- **Design**: Randomized controlled trial with independent evaluation

## Expected Outcomes

- Peer-reviewed evidence on labor market effects
- Data on health, mental health, and well-being outcomes
- Cost-benefit analysis compared to existing programs

## Funding Mechanism

Proposed funding via a 0.1% financial transaction tax, estimated to generate $18B annually.
