## How to Get Involved

The debate around Universal Basic Income is growing. Here's how you can participate:

### Attend a Town Hall
We host monthly virtual town halls where citizens, researchers, and policymakers discuss income support policy. [Register for the next event →](#)

### Share Your Story
Have you experienced economic insecurity or benefited from a cash assistance program? Your story can help shape the evidence base for future policy.

### Contact Your Representative
Use our template letter to urge your elected officials to support guaranteed income pilot legislation.

### Spread the Word
Share the [UBI Explainer](/explainers/universal-basic-income) with your networks.
