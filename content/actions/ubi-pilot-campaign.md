## Why This Campaign Matters

Guaranteed income pilots have shown promising results in Stockton, Finland, and Kenya — but the evidence base for UK and Irish populations remains thin. This campaign calls for a properly funded, independently evaluated pilot to fill that gap.

We are not asking politicians to adopt UBI. We are asking them to **test it seriously**.

## What We're Asking For

A publicly funded, independently evaluated pilot of guaranteed income (£1,000/month, 200 participants, 2-year duration) in at least one UK region and one Irish county by 2025.

The pilot must include:
- A representative cross-section of residents, not just those already in precarious work
- Independent academic evaluation with pre-registered outcomes
- A public dashboard publishing results in near-real-time

## What's Already Working

Research from the [Stockton SEED pilot](/pilots/stockton-seed-pilot) showed that cash transfers without conditions improved full-time employment, reduced income volatility, and improved mental health outcomes. Evidence from Finland's 2017–2018 experiment pointed to improved wellbeing and trust in institutions.

These results don't prove UBI works everywhere — but they make the case for taking the question seriously.
