## Overview

The Stockton Economic Empowerment Demonstration (SEED) was a 24-month guaranteed income pilot that ran from February 2019 through January 2021 in Stockton, California. 125 residents received $500/month with no strings attached.

## Key Findings

- **Full-time employment** among recipients increased from 28% to 40% (vs. 25% to 37% in control group)
- Recipients reported significantly **lower anxiety and depression**
- Most income was spent on **food, utilities, and household goods**
- No significant increase in spending on alcohol or tobacco

## Lessons

SEED demonstrated that unconditional cash transfers can improve well-being and employment outcomes without the feared negative effects on work motivation. The pilot informed subsequent guaranteed income programs across dozens of U.S. cities.
